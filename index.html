Welcome to Insync

Step 1: Creating a Document

Fill in the title, author, category, and content. You can also upload multiple photos to accompany your document.

Step 2: Viewing Documents

Browse through all documents, use the search bar to find specific content, or filter by category.

Step 3: Comments & Ratings

Click on any document to view it in full and leave comments with star ratings to help improve the content.

Step 4: Admin Features

Admins can manage passwords and create new users. Use the Admin button in the top bar to access these features.
